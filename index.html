My awesome skills.

I believe that everyone has some special 'attribute'. For me that attribute is my love for art and craft. Ordinary people look at designs and find them good or bad, but I can feel what the designer must have felt and thought of, when making the design.

Coding.

Well versed with coding languages like C, Java, HTML, CSS, jade, Sass, Less , JavaScript and android basics. Can work with Databases like MongoDB,SQL .

Softwares.

Have experience of using industry-required softwares like GitHub, Gitter, Slack, Sublime, Robomongo and various IDEs like Eclipse, GNU - CodeBlocks, Android Studio. CAD softwares like Autodesk MAYA, AutoCAD

Problem solving.

I love puzzles! I have taught myself solving 3*3*3 and 4*4*4 Rubic's cube. Puzzles help us think linearly and that is of utmost importance in the computer programming world.

Designing.

I love to decode Logos of companies, and similarly like to design logos, graphics, fonts in my free time, similarly I can design UI and animations for applications.

Note-taking.

Note - taking is an art; how to write, how to make important points easily stand out and make notes look alive! Yes, thas's my forte! I also use a concept of Mind-Mapping to clearly understand the 'Big picture'.

Perfection.

For me a job shabbily done is worse than a job not done. I bielieve in embracing the task at hand with all my enthusiasm. After all, 'The details are not the design, they make the design'
